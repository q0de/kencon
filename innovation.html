Roto-Vise

Roto-vise is an expandable bed door latch - made out of strong automotive grade plastic. Kencon is the sole supplier and producer of this patented product, allowing us to be the first to market a bed door latch that closes with such ease and safety.

"Next Gen" Lock System

Next Gen locking system comes in two different styles and is used for storage, luggage, containers, and RV safe keepings.

Kencon has put their twist on a normal locking system and has implemented technology enabling the end user to unlock the system through mobile ap technology. This revolutionary smart phone compatibility allows the end user to make sure their investments are safe.
